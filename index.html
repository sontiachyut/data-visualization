Custom Visualization : National Park and their Bio Diversities

Description

 
I have created a Treemap with nested Circular Packing, this is a Combinational Visualization.

The Treemap visualizes all the National Park and the size the rectangle marks the total number of species, 
The National Parks are further grouped into States.

The Circular Packing shows the different categories of species present for each national park.

Marks and Channels of the Visualization


Marks : Area - Treemap: (Containment Mark) Heirarchial relationship between states and national park.
Point - Circular Packing: Number of Categories.

Channels : Area - Treemap: Total Number of species present in each national park.
Circular Packing: Total Number of species present for each category.

Color -  Treemap: The State a National Park belongs to.
Circular Packing: Different Species.

                    
Three attributes visualized : State of National Park, Unique Categories, Count of each Category.
